Friend Detector App

Friend Detector app is a friend location tracking app, you can find friend just adding your friend Id in your app.

Description

  • 1. Let friends find you
  • 2. Get shortest direction
  • 3. Request Notification

Let’s face it:

Everybody has a friend who is continually late, and/or often gets lost. Let Locate and Find My Friends by letting you know where your friend is spotted progressively, so you know what time they'll arrive. It will draw a route between both locations, so you can help friend who are lost.
